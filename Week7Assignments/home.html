Come Play With Us!

We are a luxury dog resort that offers a "play-cation" for your dog while you are away or at work. We believe every dog deserves the same amount of loving care. This means, no matter their shape, size, or personality type, we provide for them all the SAME way, for the SAME price. It's that simple!

We offer overnight boarding in our Tiki Suites, while having access to play groups throughout the day for no extra charge.

In addition, we offer daycare services. Feel free to drop them off for a few hours or for our entire operating hours. Our daycare services allow your pup to come enjoy themselves while you work, leisure or just need us to provide a safe place for FUN!

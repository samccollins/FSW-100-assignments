Owners Brandon and Kayleigh are both from Upstate New York and have been apart of the Cape Coral Community since 2015. They are no strangers to hard work, and with determination opened a total of 7,200 sq. ft. of fun for your dogs! Offering services in Boarding, Daycare, and Grooming.

Being the 4th generation of entrepreneurs, it was only natural for Brandon to venture into unfamiliar territory.

This well experienced partnership would evolve to create a business that they both could enjoy doing. The man who would carry on family tradition, and a woman with a dream and pet industry experience.
